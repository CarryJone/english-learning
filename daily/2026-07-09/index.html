單句模式

Mina took the report and opened the office door. The blue line now pointed to a narrow hall. Lina was waiting by a small counter upstairs. A night guard looked at the papers in Mina's hands. "I need to deliver this now." Mina said. He looked at the report and frowned. "The front clerk leaves at 8:20," he said. Mina ran to the service window with Lina.

The clerk was putting files into a box. Mina held up the papers. "This is proof about Route 8:10." she said. "Can you read this now?" The clerk stopped and read the first page. One red line was under May Lin's name. Another record said, "One record was hidden." Lina pointed at the old station stamp.

The clerk looked at Mina and asked, "Who gave you this?" "May Lin did," Mina answered. The guard stepped back from the hall. Then the bus engine turned off outside. The service screen came up by itself. It showed one blue sentence: "Go to Platform 12." The clerk opened a locked drawer. Inside was a second map and one blue receipt. The clerk whispered, "You need the last platform key."
