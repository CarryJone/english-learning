# 📖 Daily English - 2026-03-10 - A Day at the Airport

## Article

Traveling by plane can be exciting but also a little **stressful**. When you arrive at the airport, you need to find the **check-in** counter first. At the counter, the staff will ask for your **passport** and your ticket. They will also ask if you have any **luggage** to check in.

After check-in, you need to go through **security**. You must put your bags on the belt and walk through the scanner. Remember to take out your laptop and any liquids.

Then you can find your **gate**. Check the **departure** board to see if your flight is on time. If you have time, you can shop at the **duty-free** store or grab a coffee.

When your flight is called, go to the gate and have your **boarding pass** ready. Enjoy your trip!

## 💡 Learning Tips

今天的單字都是機場必備詞彙！下次旅遊時試著用英文和機場人員溝通，例如問：「Where is gate 12?」或「Is this the check-in counter for flight CI123?」 從旅遊情境開始開口，是最自然的練習方式。
