Mina looked at the map again.Lina stayed outside the gate.The bus door was still open.A small sign lit up by the door.It said, "Route 8:10. One rider."Mina asked, "Which rider?"The driver did not answer.His voice came from inside the bus.

But Lina still saw no face.Mina held the map near the light.A dark shadow crossed Seat 12.Lina said, "Be careful, Mina."Mina looked at the printed names.May Lin was next to Seat 12.Mina Lin was next to the gate.The exact time was under both names.

It was 8:10 now."Can I see the sign again?" Mina asked.She heard no feet on the floor.The blue bus screen went black.Then one line came back.It said, "Two names. One choice."Lina called, "Mina, come back now."The driver said, "You can still leave."Mina took one slow step.
