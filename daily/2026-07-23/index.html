Reading a Café Menu and Ordering Lunch
🎯 Mission
讀懂線上咖啡店菜單並完成午餐點餐
任務：你要先看手機上的英文菜單，選出一份午餐，再在櫃檯清楚下單。
成功條件：找出套餐價格 NT$180、供應到 2:00、取餐時間 12:20；說出飲料尺寸；聽不清楚時確認 12:20 還是 12:30。
英文輸入：online café menu + counter dialogue
Survival sentence：Could I get the lunch set, please?
🗺️ Ability Focus
英文資訊判讀旅行開口日常反應
今天的能力證據：從線上菜單找出 NT$180、供應到 2:00、一份配料與 12:20 pickup；用 check the menu 先找資訊，用 Could I get ...? 點餐；飲料尺寸或時間不清楚時，用 Which size would you like? 與 Did you say ...? 完成確認。

單句：

Cafe Menu for Lunch. Mia says, “I need to check the menu first.” The lunch set costs NT$180 and is served until 2:00. A drink is included with the set. The menu says, “Choose one topping.” The soup is another option and a good choice. Mia notices the small drink is included.

At the counter, Mia says, “Could I get the lunch set, please?” The clerk asks, “Which size would you like?” Mia answers, “A small drink, please.” The clerk says, “Your order will be ready at 12:20.” Mia asks, “Sorry, did you say 12:20 or 12:30?” The clerk says, “Twelve twenty. Pick it up at the counter.” Mia keeps the number and returns at 12:20.

🔗 Key Phrases
check the menu Collocation
查看菜單
文章用法：I need to check the menu first。先看網頁、app 或紙本菜單。
included with the set Collocation
包含在套餐裡
資訊頁常用來判斷飲料、配菜或早餐是否要另外付費。
choose one topping Collocation
選一種配料
點餐時可把 one 換成 two，直接套用。
Could I get ...? Fixed Expression
我可以要……嗎？
文章用法：Could I get the lunch set, please? 外帶、咖啡店或櫃檯點餐都能說。
Which size would you like? Fixed Expression
你想要哪個尺寸／份量？
店員確認飲料、餐點或票種時的常見問法。
pick it up Phrasal Verb
把它拿回來；領取它
文章用法：pick it up at the counter → 到櫃檯取餐。
at the counter Fixed Expression
在櫃檯
可用於點餐、付款或取餐的位置確認。
Did you say ...? Fixed Expression
你剛才是說……嗎？
聽不清楚時間、尺寸或價格時，先核對關鍵資訊。

🎭 Role-play

你是顧客，店員提供資訊。先遮住答案，再說出 You 的回應；中途會遇到售罄、尺寸與時間確認。

Staff
Hello. Are you ready to order?
You
I need to check the menu first.
Staff
The lunch set is sold out now.
資訊改變：午餐套餐沒有了，店員提供 soup 作為替代選項。
You
Could I get the soup, please?
Staff
Sure. A drink is included with the soup set. Which size would you like?
You
I want to make sure: is a drink included with the set? A small drink, please.
Staff
Your order will be ready at 12:20.
You
Sorry, did you say 12:20 or 12:30?
修復句：聽不清楚時先核對數字，再問 Can I pick it up at the counter?
Staff
Twelve twenty. Yes, you can pick it up at the counter.
You
Thanks. I will come back at 12:20.

💡 Learning Tips
  • 先抓菜單上的價格、供應時間、包含項目與取餐時間；這些資訊比讀懂每個字更重要。
  • 今天最值得直接說的是 Could I get the lunch set, please?；看到 included with the set，就知道那個項目已包含在套餐裡。
  • 今天主動開口的是 check the menu 和 Could I get ...?；included with the set 主要用來看懂菜單資訊。聽不清楚時先說 Did you say ...?。
