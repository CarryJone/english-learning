單句模式

Mina pushed the hidden door open. A small office was behind the wall. One lamp was on over an old desk. A woman in a blue uniform stood by the map. The driver closed the door and stayed outside. The woman looked tired but calm. She said, "My name is May Lin." Mina held the folded map very tight.

"Then M.L. was you," Mina said. May nodded and showed her an old report. "I tried to report the last blue route," she said. "But the front office hid it years ago." Mina saw a missing stop marked in red. The sign on the page said, "Return path only." "Why did you send the messages to me?" Mina asked. "Because you read every sign," May answered.

"And you still ask when you are afraid." Mina looked at the office door. "What do you need now?" she asked. May pointed to the report and the route map. "Please take these to the front office." she said. "The night guard will leave in ten minutes." Mina took a slow breath and picked up the papers. Outside, the blue bus engine started again. On the screen above the door, one more line came up: "Go before 8:20."
