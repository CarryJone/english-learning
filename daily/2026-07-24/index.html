Checking a Store Return Policy
🎯 Mission
讀懂退換貨規則並換一件合身的衣服
你在旅行中買了一件衣服。先看手機上的商店規則，再向店員詢問換尺寸；如果缺貨，要能選擇退款或其他商品。
成功條件：找出 30 天期限與收據／吊牌要求；說出換大一號；遇到缺貨時確認退款或店內購物金。
英文輸入：線上退換貨規則 + 店內短對話

📖 Article
今日單字 複習單字 重要片語 🔊 點擊播放單句 單句：

Mina checks a store's return policy on her phone. Items can be returned within thirty days with the receipt. Please keep the label on the item. Sale items can only be exchanged, not refunded.

Mina bought a shirt yesterday, but it does not fit. She goes back to the store and speaks with a clerk. Mina: Could I exchange this for a larger size? Clerk: Sure. Let me check the stock.

Clerk: Sorry, the larger size is out of stock today. Mina: Sorry, did you say refund or store credit? Clerk: You can get a refund with the receipt. Mina: Could I get store credit instead? Clerk: The blue shirt is another choice. Mina checks the label before taking it.

🔗 Key Phrases
return policy Collocation
退換貨規則
在網站或店內規則中找期限與條件；今天主要用來看懂資訊。
within thirty days Fixed Expression
在三十天內
看到期限時直接抓整塊，不必逐字翻譯。
keep the label on Phrasal Verb
把吊牌留在上面
keep + 物品 + on，常用來提醒衣物或包裝的狀態。
goes back to Phrasal Verb
回到
goes back to the store = 回到商店處理問題。
check the stock Collocation
查庫存
向店員確認某個尺寸或商品還有沒有。
out of stock Fixed Expression
缺貨；沒有庫存
資訊目標語塊：看到它就知道要找替代品或改變安排。
exchange this for a larger size Fixed Expression
把這件換成大一號
直接套用的購物 survival sentence：Could I exchange this for a larger size?
store credit Collocation
店內購物金
退款以外的常見替代方案，可接 instead 或 as an option。

💡 Learning Tips
  • 今天最值得直接背的是 Could I exchange this for a larger size?；遇到缺貨就先說 out of stock，再問替代方案。
  • 看退換貨頁面時，先圈出期限、收據、吊牌與特價品限制；return policy 是很常見的查資料關鍵詞。
  • 任務成功的標準是：能說出換尺寸、能確認退款／購物金，並在資訊改變時問 Sorry, did you say ...?
