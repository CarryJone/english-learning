單句模式

Mina and Lina reached Platform 12. The platform was dark but clean. A blue sign blinked above the wall. It said, "Insert the last key." Mina held the small key in her hand. The clerk stayed behind the glass door. "Do not unlock it alone." he said. Lina stood close to Mina.

"We open it together." Lina said. Mina put the key into a small box. The box clicked and showed a final screen. The screen asked, "Choose the return bus." Mina saw two times on the screen. One time was 8:10. The other time was tomorrow. May Lin's name appeared under tomorrow.

Mina's name appeared under 8:10. Mina took a slow breath. "Can we change the names?" she asked. The blue receipt moved in her pocket. A new line was printed there. It said, "Return one rider, not two." Lina looked at Mina and shook her head. The platform lights turned blue. Then May Lin's voice said, "Use your name."
