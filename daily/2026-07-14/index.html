單句模式

Mina typed her full name on the blue screen. The screen began to switch between two names. Lina held the key. "What should we do now?" Lina asked. A new line appeared under Mina's name. It said, "Mina will stay here." A second line appeared under May Lin's name. It said, "One rider can return."

Mina showed proof from the old report. "Can this bus bring May Lin back?" she asked. "Yes, but you must stay here." Mina looked at the final map. A blue path led from Platform 12 to the bus. The blue bus arrived without a sound. Its door opened, but Mina did not move. "I will wait." Mina said. Lina stood beside her. May Lin got off the bus.

She looked tired, but she smiled. "Thank you for using your name," May said. The screen changed one last time. It said, "Route closed. Return complete." Then the blue bus and the blue line disappeared. Mina checked the receipt in her pocket. It was blank at last. But one small word appeared: "Remember."

stay here
Fixed Expression

Fixed Expression

Route closed. Return complete.
Fixed Expression

看螢幕或公共系統的結果提示時，先抓住 closed 和 complete。

💡 Learning Tips
