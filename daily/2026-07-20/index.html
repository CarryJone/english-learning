🎯 Mission
看懂車站路線並確認正確出口
任務：你要從車站地圖、告示與站務對話找出前往 City Library 的路線，並在出口資訊改變時重新確認。
成功條件：找出 Green Line、Platform 2、Exit 4 與開放時間；跟著指示走到正確出口；說出 Which way should I go for Exit 4?
英文輸入：station map + exit notice + information-desk dialogue

🔊 點擊播放單句
單句：

Mina is at Central Station and wants to reach the City Library. The station map says, “Take the Green Line to Platform 2.” From Platform 2, she should follow the blue signs to Exit 4. The route goes upstairs, then turns left near the ticket machines. Mina checks the exact exit number on her phone. A notice says Exit 4 is open until 10 p.m. She walks to the information desk to ask for directions.

“Excuse me, which way should I go for Exit 4?” The staff member says, “Go straight, then turn right after the stairs.” Mina hears “Exit 3” and is not sure. “Sorry, I didn't catch that. Could you say the exit again?” The staff member points to a green sign and repeats, “Exit 4.” Mina follows the sign and reaches the library safely. If Exit 4 is closed, Exit 5 is another option.

🔗 Key Phrases
follow the signs Collocation
跟著標誌走
文章用法：follow the blue signs → 依照藍色標誌前進。
go upstairs Phrasal Verb
上樓
文章用法：The route goes upstairs → 路線要往樓上走。
go straight Fixed Expression
直走
問路時可直接套用：Go straight, then turn right。
turn right Collocation
右轉
文章用法：turn right after the stairs → 過樓梯後右轉。
ask for directions Collocation
問路
迷路或不確定出口時，可說 ask for directions。
I didn't catch that Fixed Expression
我沒聽清楚
聽不懂時間、出口或月台時，先用這句請對方重說。
another option Fixed Expression
另一個選項
原路線關閉或改變時，用來詢問替代方案。
