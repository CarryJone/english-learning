今天的能力證據：從交通公告找出 Blue Line、8:30 train、replacement bus 與 Stop C；向工作人員確認 Does the replacement bus go to Central Station?；資訊改變或聽不清楚時，用 I didn't catch that 和 Where should I go now? 修復對話。
🎯 Mission
讀懂交通公告並確認替代巴士
任務：你要先讀懂手機上的列車服務公告，再到車站確認替代巴士的正確上車位置。
成功條件：找出受影響的路線、取消的班次與公告上的月台；聽到現場位置改成 Stop C 後重新確認；說出 Does the replacement bus go to Central Station?。
英文輸入：train service alert + station sign + staff dialogue
Survival sentence：Does the replacement bus go to Central Station?

今日單字
複習單字
重要片語
🔊 點擊播放單句
單句：

On Saturday morning, I check the city train app before leaving. A new notice says the Blue Line is affected by track work. The 8:30 train to Central Station is cancelled today. The app says a replacement bus leaves from Platform 4. Passengers should arrive ten minutes early and show the same ticket. I want to make sure I understand the change.

At the station, I look for the replacement bus sign. I ask, “Does the replacement bus go to Central Station?” The staff member says, “Yes, but it picks up passengers at Stop C.” “I didn't catch that, so I ask, ‘Where should I go now?’” The staff points ahead and says, “Turn left after the stairs.” I choose the replacement bus instead and arrive on time.

🔗 Key Phrases
be affected by Collocation
受到……影響
公告用法：The Blue Line is affected by track work → 藍線受到軌道工程影響。
be cancelled Fixed Expression
被取消
看到班次消失或取消時，可說 The train is cancelled。
a replacement bus Collocation
接駁巴士
交通改道時，replacement bus 是常見的替代交通說法。
check the notice Collocation
查看公告
出發前先 check the notice，找出時間、地點與限制。
make sure Fixed Expression
確認；確保
I want to make sure I understand → 我想確認自己有理解。
look for Phrasal Verb
尋找
到現場找標誌或櫃檯時，可以說 look for the sign。
pick up Phrasal Verb
接載；接人
The bus picks up passengers at Stop C → 巴士在 C 站接乘客。
I didn't catch that Fixed Expression
我沒聽清楚
聽不清楚位置、時間或號碼時，先用這句請對方重說。

💡 Learning Tips
  • 先抓公告的三個核心：哪條路線受影響、哪班車取消、替代交通在哪裡。
  • 現場資訊改變時，不要只說 “Sorry”；用 I didn't catch that 接著問 Where should I go now?。
  • 確認後把關鍵資訊重複一次：Stop C、ten minutes early、the same ticket。
