🗺️ Ability Focus
旅行開口英文資訊判讀日常反應
今天的能力證據：從旅館資訊頁找出 breakfast time、Floor 2、checkout time 與 luggage storage；用 Can I leave my luggage here after checkout? 詢問寄放；資訊改成五點時，用 Did you say five or six? 與 I want to make sure 完成確認。
🎯 Mission
讀懂旅館資訊並詢問退房後行李寄放
任務：你要先讀旅館資訊頁，找出早餐與退房規則，再向櫃檯詢問退房後能否寄放行李。
成功條件：找出早餐時間與樓層、退房時間、行李寄放地點；聽到領取時間從六點改成五點後重新確認；說出 Can I leave my luggage here after checkout?
英文輸入：hotel guest information page + reception dialogue
Survival sentence：Can I leave my luggage here after checkout?

🔊 點擊播放單句
單句：

Hotel Guest Information. Breakfast is included with every stay. It is served from 7:00 to 10:00. The breakfast room is on Floor 2. Guests must check out by 11:00. Luggage storage is available at reception.

Mia reads the page and speaks to the receptionist. “Can I leave my luggage here after checkout?” “Yes, we can store your luggage until 6:00 p.m.” “Please pick it up at reception.” “Sorry, did you say six or seven?” “Six o'clock, and storage is free.” “I want to make sure breakfast is included.” “Yes, and bring your luggage tag later.”

🔗 Key Phrases
breakfast is included Collocation · 今日目標
早餐包含在費用內
資訊頁常見用法：Breakfast is included with every stay。看到 included，要判斷是否需要另外付費。
Can I leave my luggage here? Fixed Expression · 今日目標
我可以把行李寄放在這裡嗎？
退房前後、博物館或車站寄物時都能直接套用。
I want to make sure Fixed Expression · 今日目標
我想確認一下
聽到時間或規則改變時，先用這句再重複關鍵資訊。
check out Phrasal Verb
退房
文章用法：Guests must check out by 11:00 → 住客必須在十一點前退房。
at reception Fixed Expression
在接待櫃檯
旅館資訊常用位置說法；美式英文也常說 at the front desk。
store your luggage Collocation
寄放你的行李
store 在這裡是動詞，和 luggage 搭配表示安全寄放。
pick up Phrasal Verb
領取；拿回
文章用法：pick it up at reception → 到接待櫃檯領回行李。
Did you say ...? Fixed Expression
你剛才是說……嗎？
沒聽清楚數字、時間或地點時，用來核對兩個可能答案。

💡 Learning Tips
  • 資訊頁先抓三件事：早餐是否包含、供應時間與地點、行李寄放到幾點。
  • 今天最值得直接說的是 Can I leave my luggage here after checkout?；看到 breakfast is included，就知道早餐不需另外加購。
  • 時間改變時用 Did you say five or six?，最後再用 I want to make sure 重複關鍵資訊。
