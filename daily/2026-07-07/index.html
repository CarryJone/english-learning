單句模式

Mina stopped at the gate. The bus light turned blue again. The sign now showed two arrows. One arrow said, "Board here." The other said, "Safe exit." Lina read the words from outside. "Mina, follow the safe exit," she said. Mina looked at the paper ticket.

The red shadow was still over Seat 12. "Should I leave now?" Mina asked. The driver answered, "You must decide." "If I go in, what happens?" Mina asked. He said, "You can help her." Mina checked the old post on the phone. One new line came up. It said, "May Lin asked for a safe way back."

Lina said, "This is not only a warning." Mina took one step back. Then the bus speaker turned on. A woman said, "Please follow the blue line." A thin blue line lit up on the floor. It went from the gate to the exit wall. Mina did not get on the bus. She followed the line and found a hidden door. Behind it, someone softly said, "Thank you for coming."
