Choosing a Ferry Departure
🗺️ Ability Focus
英文資訊判讀公共英文 旅行開口日常反應
今天的能力證據：先讀時刻表找出 Dock 2、9:20 與 10:10；看到 sold out 後詢問替代班次；資訊改變時用 Where should I go now? 確認下一步。
🎯 Mission
讀懂渡輪時刻並問替代班次
任務：你要從渡輪票務頁和櫃檯對話找出時間、碼頭與票價；第一班售罄時，改選另一班船。
成功條件：找出 9:20 / 10:10 登船時間、Dock 2、NT$180 單程票價；問到替代班次；說出 Is the 10:10 boat still available?
英文輸入：ferry timetable + ticket notice + counter dialogue
📖 Article
今日單字
複習單字
重要片語
🔊 點擊播放單句
單句：

May wants to get to Island Market before lunch. She will check the timetable on her phone. The first boat leaves from Dock 2 at 9:20 a.m. The next boarding time is 10:10 a.m. A one-way ticket costs NT$180. The nearby station is one block from the dock.

May plans to arrive early at the counter. The clerk says, “Sorry, the 9:20 boat is sold out.” May asks, “Is the 10:10 boat still available?” The clerk says, “Yes, but boarding starts at 9:55.” May asks, “Is there another option if we miss it?”

The clerk points to a later departure at 11:30. May says, “Okay, we can take that one instead.” She saves the ticket and plans to arrive early. The boat returns at 4:30 p.m., so the plan works. The page says, “Check the return time before you pay.”

🔗 Key Phrases
get to
Phrasal Verb
到達；前往
文章用法：get to Island Market → 前往 Island Market。
check the timetable
Collocation
查看時刻表
出發前先查時間，再決定要搭哪一班。
one-way ticket
Collocation
單程票
看到票價時，注意 one-way 是單程，不是來回。
arrive early
Collocation
提早到
登船、登機或有時間限制的活動都能直接使用。
at the counter
Fixed Expression
在櫃檯
買票、問問題或確認替代方案時的常見位置說法。
sold out
Fixed Expression
售罄、賣完
看到票、房間或活動名額已經沒有時，先辨識這個限制。
take ... instead
Fixed Expression
改搭……；改選……
原本的選項不能用時，詢問或說明替代方案。
